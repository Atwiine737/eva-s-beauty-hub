Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Click the "Place New Order" button, fill in your details, select your product, and submit. You'll receive an order confirmation on WhatsApp.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa (Direct STK Push), Mobile Money transfers to +256 0752546261 (A. Evalyne), and payments via WhatsApp for more flexible options. Choose your preferred method at checkout!

How do I pay with M-Pesa?

Select "M-Pesa" at checkout, enter your phone number, and you'll receive a prompt on your phone to enter your PIN. Once confirmed, your order is automatically updated!

What if I don't receive the M-Pesa prompt?

Ensure your phone is on, has a stable signal, and isn't in "Do Not Disturb" mode. If it still doesn't appear, try again or contact us on WhatsApp for assistance.

How long does delivery take?

We offer fast delivery! Orders within Kampala are typically delivered within 24-48 hours. For locations outside Kampala, delivery may take 2-3 business days.

Are your products authentic?

Yes! All our products are 100% authentic, sourced directly from verified suppliers and meet international quality standards.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Contact us on WhatsApp immediately after placing your order. We can help modify or cancel orders before they're processed for shipping.

What is your return policy?

Due to hygiene reasons, we only accept returns for items that are damaged or incorrect. Please notify us within 24 hours of delivery if there's any issue with your order.

What if I have a skin reaction to a product?

We recommend doing a patch test before full use. If you experience irritation, discontinue use immediately and consult a professional. You can also reach out for product-specific advice.

Do you offer wholesale options?

Yes! We provide competitive wholesale pricing for bulk orders. Contact us on WhatsApp with your requirements for a custom quote.

Do you offer beauty consultations?

Yes! We offer personalized beauty consultations. Contact us via WhatsApp to book a session with our expert consultants.
