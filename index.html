Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse our products, add items to your cart, and click "Place New Order". Fill in your details and select your preferred payment method. You can also order directly via WhatsApp!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Mobile Money (M-Pesa) and Cash on Delivery. Pay via M-Pesa to +256 752546261 or pay in cash when your order arrives.

What are the delivery charges?

Free delivery on orders over UGX 100,000! Standard delivery within Kampala is UGX 10,000. Delivery to other regions in Uganda varies.

How do I pay with M-Pesa?

Select Mobile Money at checkout. You'll receive instructions to send payment to +256 752546261 via M-Pesa. Use your Order ID as the payment reference.

How do I track my order?

Use your Order ID on the Orders page to track your order status. Orders show as Pending (new), Processing (1-24hrs), Shipped (24-72hrs), or Delivered (72hrs+).

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for unused items in original packaging. Contact us for returns. We want you to be completely satisfied!

Do you offer wholesale prices?

Yes! Contact us on WhatsApp for bulk orders and wholesale pricing. We cater to resellers and salonners across Uganda.

How do I book a makeup service?

Contact us via WhatsApp or call +256 752546261 to book your makeup appointment. We service weddings, parties, photoshoots, and special events.
